Scenario: An online pet store wants to engage and retain users via email communication and increase purchases. To achieve this goal, a Welcome Email Series was created. An example of a welcome journey and several emails from the welcome series are shown below.

Welcome Email Journey

Welcome email

Subject line: Nancy, welcome to BARK! Let's find something for your friend.

Tone of voice: A cute dog named Bark communicates with customers in a friendly manner.

Segment: Users received a welcome email 1 minute after signing up.

An email written concisely so as not to distract users from the main points: product categories and free shipping.

When customers subscribe to social media or download a mobile app, they earn Pet Points that are easily redeemed for a discount.

HTML email

Discount for the 1st purchase

Subject line: Nancy, shop now and we’ll pay 20% of your purchase!

Segment: Registered users who have not made a purchase will receive this email 3 hours after registration.

The lead receives a promo code with a 20% discount on the first purchase. The discount ends in 7 days.

The main purpose of the email is to provide a useful offer to leads and highlight free shipping. If the leads subscribes to social networks, they will receive Pet Points that can easily be exchanged for a discount.

HTML email

Loyalty program starts from the 3rd purchase

Subject line: Thanks Nancy! Get your perks with the BARK loyalty program.

Segment: Clients will receive a message about the loyalty program 1 minute after their second purchase.

The email pushes customers to make their next purchase by persuading them to become members of the BARK loyalty program and receive discounts of 10%-20% depending on the purchase amount.

HTML email

Survey email

Subject line: Just one question, Nancy

Segment: If a customer has just registered and has not made a purchase on the 7th day after registration, they will receive a survey email.

The main idea of the email is to interact with customers by asking about their house pets. After providing an answer, the customer receives a Pet Point, which can be exchanged for a discount on a purchase.
